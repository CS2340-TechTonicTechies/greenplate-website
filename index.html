Introducing GreenPlate

Welcome to GreenPlate: a sustainable food management system.

Our app contains many features to aid our users in their health journey. Utilizing a global, user-made recipe list, virtual ingredient pantry, generated shopping list, personally set calorie goals, calorie tracking, and many other features, our app aims to make managing a user’s food intake quicker and simpler through a sleek user interface.

On opening the app, our GreenPlate welcome screen appears, greeting the user with a custom design. A brief transition through the login subsequently takes you to our home screen.

From here, a user can access any of our features: Input Meals which allows for meal input and multiple calorie visualizations, Recipes which displays a global recipe database and allows you to upload your own recipe, Ingredients which displays your current pantry of ingredients, and Shopping which facilitates an easy-access, integrated place to create your list and add all the ingredients from a recipe. These are just a few of the many features in our app that help to provide a quick and convenient meal tracker.

In Review

GreenPlate is a collaborative project with the intent to create and design an app that promotes sustainable food management for its users; we believe we have achieved that goal. Throughout the design and coding process, we faced many challenges: version control, tight deadlines, etc. Nevertheless, through our group efforts, we have come together to make the app you see demoed here. There are a lot of moving parts integrated into an intuitive, easy-to-use design.

All of our team members contributed to the app as needed during the span of two-week sprints. Under the given time constraints, we realized how interdependent our tasks and features were which thus changed our workstyle and influenced our scheduling as well. Overall, we learned how to work better as a team through productive in-person and online meetings to accomplish our goals.
